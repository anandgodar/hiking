## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

### ‚ùå Don't:
1. **Keyword Stuff** - Write naturally for humans first
2. **Copy Content** - Always original, even if AI-assisted
3. **Ignore Mobile** - 70%+ traffic will be mobile
4. **Forget Alt Text** - Every single image needs it
5. **Skip Analytics** - "If you can't measure it, you can't improve it"
6. **Buy Links** - Google will penalize you
7. **Neglect Speed** - 3+ second load time = higher bounce rate
8. **Ignore UX** - Good SEO means good user experience

### ‚úÖ Do:
1. **Focus on User Intent** - Answer the question they're really asking
2. **Update Content Regularly** - Fresh content signals quality
3. **Internal Linking** - Link related trails together
4. **Build Relationships** - Outreach is about partnerships, not begging
5. **Be Patient** - SEO takes 3-6 months to show results
6. **Double Down on What Works** - If waterfall posts rank, make more
7. **Engage Your Community** - Reply to every comment/email
8. **Stay Consistent** - 3 good posts per week > 10 mediocre posts per month
